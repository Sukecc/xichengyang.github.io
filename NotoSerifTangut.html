Cultural Preservation via Open-Source Infrastructure.

As the main designer, I led the modification of Monotype’s previous version, encompassing a character set of 6,915 characters. With support from Tangut scholars, we corrected wrong characters and improved font quality. This project highlights my design expertise and passion for cultural preservation.
