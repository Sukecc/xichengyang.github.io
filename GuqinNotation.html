Traditional Chinese Music Notation Digital Infrastructure.

The project is a precedent for typeface design without codepoints, counting over 200,000 reduced character forms, following ancient norms for strokes and structure, and designed in a unified standard. It is also the only character database project development for the international encoding of the notation system for Guqin and will be used in the future.
