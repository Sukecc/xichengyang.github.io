About & Practice

Xicheng Yang is an award-winning typeface designer and researcher from China, currently based in the UK. She graduated with distinction from the MA in Typeface Design at the University of Reading, where she subsequently completed her doctorate.

Her specialized research bridges historical script analysis with contemporary digital production, focusing on the development of digital typefaces for the Tangut script—an extinct writing system used in northwestern China between the 11th and 13th centuries.

Specialising in Chinese, Tangut, Latin scripts and complex multi-script type design and typography, Xicheng’s practice focuses on multi-script visual harmony, meticulous glyph craftsmanship, and systemic typeface expansion across retail and custom typeface projects.
