An Exploration of Contemporary Kai Style Duality.

HanJiangYaKai is an ongoing experimental typeface project that re-examines the structured elegance and kinetic tension of traditional Hanzi script. Merging classic calligraphic weight with sharp geometric refinement, the concept challenges conventional script proportions, exploring new spatial dynamics within a highly curating limited glyph repository.
